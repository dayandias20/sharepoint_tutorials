Problem-Solving

Migrating 15-year-old SharePoint 2010 (1200+ workflows, custom code) to Online. Challenges: legacy workflows incompatible, deep folder nesting. Solution: phased migration, rebuilt workflows in Power Automate, flattened structure, change management with champions. 6 months, zero data loss.

51 Tell me about a time when a critical website issue occurred during business hours.

Course registration page crashed 30 min before opening. Infinite loop in JavaScript. Immediately disabled script, restored basic functionality, communicated via banner, fixed bug (missing null check), deployed 45 minutes later. Students registered on time.

52 Share an example of a workflow automation that didn't work as expected.

Approval workflow randomly skipped second approver. Found issue: null value when optional field blank. Added condition checking if empty, made field required when needed. Tested 10 scenarios, no failures since.

53 Describe a technical problem that initially stumped you.

Search results delayed 6-8 hours. Checked crawls, index—looked fine. After 2 days, discovered content approval enabled but no one approving, keeping items in "Pending." Disabled approval, re-crawled. Lesson: check basics first.
